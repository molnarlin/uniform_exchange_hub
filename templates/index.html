About Us

This website is dedicated to exchanging pre-loved school uniforms across the UK. It’s completely free to use and register. As a charity, our mission is to connect parents, school uniform banks, and schools to facilitate the exchange of school uniforms, reduce landfill waste, support the environment, and help families budget for the next school year. On this page, you can search for your local school and find uniforms specific to it. If you have school uniforms to donate, you can register here. Please note, this platform is not for selling items, although some organizations do accept monetary donations. If you find this site helpful, please share it on your social media!

Why register with Us?

By registering, users become part of a community dedicated to mutual support. This fosters a sense of belonging and cooperation among parents, schools, and uniform banks. Registration simplifies the donation process, allowing users to list items they wish to donate, making it easier for others to find and request them. By participating in the program, users help reduce landfill waste and promote sustainable practices, contributing to environmental protection. Additionally, families can save money by accessing free or low-cost uniforms, significantly easing the financial burden of purchasing new school attire each year.
